# Behaviours 

 

## Changing and Improving 

* Lead continuous improvement of database services and supporting processes, reducing manual effort and risk. 
* Identify legacy constraints and technical debt, proposing incremental modernisation approaches that minimise disruption while improving outcomes. 
* Encourage and support delivery teams to adopt improved engineering practices to strengthen operability in live. 
* Drive standardisation where it increases reliability and value for money, while remaining pragmatic to product delivery constraints. 
 

## Making Effective Decisions 

* Produce evidence-based architecture options and recommendations, clearly articulating trade-offs across cost, risk, technical debt, performance and resilience. 
* Define and assure non-functional requirements and ensure designs meet agreed standards. 
* Identify architectural risks early, recommending mitigations and prioritised remediation plans. 
* Contribute to business cases, options appraisals and service readiness assessments with clear technical rationale, dependencies and long-term sustainability considerations. 
 

## Leadership, Communicating and Influencing 

* Explain complex technical designs and risks in clear, outcome-focused language appropriate for senior stakeholders, delivery teams and non-technical audiences. 
* Provide constructive challenge and assurance through governance forums, enabling sound decisions and shared ownership. 
* Build strong partnerships across product, engineering, operations, security, data protection and suppliers to align delivery with architectural intent. 
* Produce and maintain high-quality architecture artefacts to support delivery and assurance. 
 

## Seeing the Big Picture 

* Ensure database and platform architecture aligns to RoS corporate strategy and the Digital, Data & Technology roadmap, anticipating future service and data needs. 
* Develop and maintain architectural roadmaps that sequence modernisation, technical debt reduction and platform evolution across products and domains. 
* Promote reuse of shared patterns, components and services to reduce duplication and enable consistent, scalable digital delivery. 
* Horizon-scan emerging technology, regulatory and security considerations, translating them into clear architectural implications and recommendations
