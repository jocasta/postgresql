## How You Should Present (Framework)

Use this every time:

1. Context & objectives
2. Assumptions & constraints
3. Current risks / challenges
4. Architecture options (2–3)
5. Trade-offs (cost, risk, debt, operability)
6. Recommended approach
7. Phased roadmap
8. Governance & assurance model
